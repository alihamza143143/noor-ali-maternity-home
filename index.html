Welcome

Trusted maternity care, close to home

At Noor Ali Clinic & Maternity Home, we believe every mother deserves safe, gentle and respectful care — at every stage of pregnancy and beyond. Located in the heart of Sardar Market on Kallar Syedan Road, our clinic has cared for mothers and babies of the local community with warmth and dedication.

Under the care of Dr. Noor Ali, Gynecologist, we provide complete maternity and women's health services in a clean, private and comforting environment. Our doors stay open 24 hours a day for emergencies — because a mother's wellbeing can never wait.

Our Services

Complete care for mother & baby

From your first check-up to a safe delivery and your newborn's first days — we are with you at every step.

🤰

Antenatal & Maternity Care

Regular pregnancy check-ups, monitoring and guidance to keep mother and baby healthy throughout pregnancy.

👶

Normal Deliveries

Safe, supported normal deliveries in a calm and hygienic environment with attentive care before and after birth.

🩺

Gynecology Consultation

Expert care for women's health concerns, menstrual issues, infections and general gynecological wellbeing.

🌸

Family Planning

Confidential counselling and guidance on family planning, tailored to each family's needs and comfort.

🍼

Newborn (Baby) Care

Gentle care and check-ups for your newborn, with advice for new mothers on feeding and early care.

🚑

24/7 Emergency Care

Maternity emergencies don't keep office hours — and neither do we. Reach us any time, day or night.
